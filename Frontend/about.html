About iLOC

What is iLOC?

iLOC (Superstore Item Locator) is an innovative solution designed to help customers easily locate items in large superstores. Our application eliminates the hassle of searching aisles by providing precise directions to the product you're looking for. Simply search for an item, and iLOC will guide you to its exact location within seconds.

Key Features

  • Real-time item tracking within the store.
  • Accurate aisle and shelf location details.
  • User-friendly interface for quick searches.
  • Seamless integration with store inventory systems.
  • Available on both mobile and web platforms.

Our Vision

At iLOC, our mission is to revolutionize the shopping experience by making it as simple and efficient as possible. We aim to enhance customer satisfaction by saving time and effort in finding items in large superstores.
